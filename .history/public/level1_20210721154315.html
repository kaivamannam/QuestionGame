Theory Guide

In this theory guide, you will learn about Bloomâ€™s taxonomy and Depth of Knowledge. In simple terms, a taxonomy means a classification system. Benjamin Bloom created a hierarchical model with six levels of complexity. The higher the level, the tougher it is to think about it and come up with answers. The sequence of cognitive skills are Remembering, Understanding, Applying, Analysing, Evaluating, and Creating. The upper three levels (Create, Evaluate and Analyse) form the HOTS (Higher Order Thinking Skills). The bottom three levels(Remember, Understand and Apply) form the LOTS (Lower Order Thinking Skills). You should develop the ability to generate questions based on HOTS levels.
